Yu-Gi-Oh: Duel Monsters

Yu-Gi-Oh is a card game based on a Japanese comic. Opponents duel against one another in an attempt to reduce the other's Life Points (LP) to 0. At the start of a duel, each duelist typically starts with 8,000 LP. However, this value can be increased/decreased if both duelists agree to it beforehand. In order to eliminate an opponent's LP, duelists play a combination of monster, spell, and trap cards. While some magic & trap cards have effects that can reduce an opponent's LP, monster cards are better suited for this purpose. This webpage is to be viewed as a Wiki that exaplains the very basics of Yu-Gi-Oh, as we will dive into monster cards, spell cards, and trap cards, as well as their many variants.
